Tables2

Work in tables. Share as text

Time saving formatting

Tables are often the best way to capture and organize information in our digital lives but are difficult to share when you want to consolidate that inforamtion as a message for a Slack or Email

Tables2 takes a GDoc with tables, captures the information and exports into a new doc so you easily share that information without wrought copy and pasting

(Some) Features

Make Lists

Identify headers and tables and export them to an easy to read list to share.

Export JSON

Use export the information in tables to JSON to easily move between ideas and requirements and implementation/testing

Build for Orgs

Identify Jira links and enhace with your company's root URL for quick linking. Find links related to people and replace them with an @ to make copy, pasting, and posting to Slack effortless.
