Tidi

Keep your Mac Clean and Organized

So many files so, little time

Download folders can become dumping grounds for all the files you get from Slack, email, etc.Tidi helps tame the beast of your Download (or other folders) by providing timely reminders and an easy interface.

(Some) Features

Drag and Drop

Select files and move them to their destination quickly and easily. Default folders make it easy to start organizing as soon as Tidi opens.

Reminders

Set a reminder to get notified on the time and dates of your choosing. Just click on a notification to start Tiding up.

QuickDrop

Select frequently needed folders to quickly move files by dragging and dropping or using hotkeys.
